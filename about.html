About

Mariesa Dale, Product Designer + Illustrator

Mariesa Dale is an award-winning, Product Designer who pursues research driven design thinking and has a hunger for tackling complex problems. An excellent collaborator, she strives to drive the evaluation of new and existing user experiences in products by incorporating feedback and best practices in order to continuously evolve and improve results. Involved in the design community since 2005, Mariesa’s clients have included Pivotal Labs, The World Bank, Marriott International, USA Today, The Kennedy Center, SpaceX, World Wildlife Fund, and various US government agencies. In 2013 she opened Washington, DC’s first mobile device testing lab, a resource used by engineers and start ups around the city to ensure the quality of their products before going to production. Originally from Seattle, she moved to the DC Metro area in 2005 after completing her Bachelor of Fine Arts at Cornish College of the Arts.
